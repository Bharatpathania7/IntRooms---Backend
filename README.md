# 🏠 IntRooms – Student Accommodation & Room Rental Platform

A modern room discovery platform that helps students and working professionals find rental rooms, PGs, hostels, and apartments near their preferred locations.

IntRooms simplifies the house-hunting experience by connecting property owners with potential tenants through a centralized and easy-to-use platform.

---

# 📌 Overview

Finding affordable and verified accommodation can be challenging, especially for students moving to a new city.

IntRooms provides a marketplace where users can:

* Search for available rooms and PGs
* Explore rental properties
* Connect directly with property owners
* Compare accommodation options
* Discover nearby housing opportunities

The platform is designed to make room hunting simple, transparent, and efficient.
